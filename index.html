Application Guidelines

In most cases, customers will come to us asking for a conventional aeration system, unless it is a project driven by energy savings, such as a state funded energy grant, or an ESCO contract where energy savings drive the project. In most cases, we have to present the high efficiency alternative.
Power Consumption Reference: For every 1000 SCFM at 8 PSIG, consider 50 HP or the equivalent in metric values. For HDLF the target is to save 20% of power consumption.
Good Candidates for HDLF Upgrade:
• Complete Mix Reactor: Single diffuser density of 7-8%. HDLF design at 20-25% density may offer 20% improvement in oxygen transfer efficiency. Recommended product: Satellite
• BNR with Tapered Aeration: First aerobic zone not already at 25% density with 9" discs. If at 15-20% density, apply ECD215 which has a slightly higher target airflow range than Satellite. Recommended product: ECD215
